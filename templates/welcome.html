Lucid Particle Design

Introduction

Lucid Particle design is a living project that helps us prototype quickly, iterate fast over new design concepts, and show interactive mockups to customers.

Designers

Use the components to make quick interactive prototoypes. Head over the the Angular Snippets tab to learn quick ways to create interactions.

Developers

Use the transitions and interactions of each component as a guide to help you code. Note: we are designers, not developers, copy code at your own risk.
